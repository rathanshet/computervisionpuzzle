How to Play

  1. Enable your webcam and make sure your hand is visible.
  2. Hover your index finger over a puzzle piece on the screen.
  3. Pinch your index finger and thumb together to grab the piece.
  4. Drag the piece to its target slot and release your pinch to drop/snap it in place!
  • Position a physical object inside the central circular target reticle.
  • Try to match the Target Color shown in the panel.
  • Hold the object steady. When the similarity reaches 85% or above, the match will trigger!
  • Solve as many colors as possible before the timer runs out.
